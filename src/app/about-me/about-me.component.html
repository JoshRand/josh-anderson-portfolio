I went to University of Houston - Clear Lake (UHCL) to study Computer Engineering. This project used a combination of software and hardware to realize the final product and was called "Smart Treadmill" the project was a success as well as a class favorite. I completed all of the funcitonality that I set out to accomplish when starting this project.

Although the project is completed, I still revisit it periodically to add new methods, funcitonality, and UI/UX updates.
